Where Ancient Trails Meet Open Sky

The Superstition Wilderness and the parks surrounding Mesa offer some of Arizona's most dramatic hiking — volcanic ridgelines, spring wildflowers, petroglyphs etched by the Hohokam, and distant views of the Valley of the Sun. These five trails span a range of difficulties so every desert wanderer finds their path.

Before You Hike

Desert Trail Essentials

💧

Hydrate Generously

Plan for at least 1 liter per hour in summer heat. Arizona sun is unforgiving — electrolytes matter as much as water volume.

🌅

Start at Dawn

On strenuous trails, aim for the trailhead by 6–7 AM from May through October. Temperatures can exceed 110°F by midday.

🦂

Watch Your Step

Shake out shoes and check rocky ledges before sitting. Rattlesnakes and scorpions are common but rarely aggressive when left alone.

🗺

Download Offline Maps

Cell service is unreliable in the Superstition Wilderness. Download AllTrails or Gaia GPS maps before you leave the trailhead.
